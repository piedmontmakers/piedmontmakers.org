This morning FIRST and LEGO Education announced that they're ending their partnership after nearly 30 years. The **2026–2027 season will be the last** one they do together. After that, both organizations plan to go their own way, each developing independent programs.

## What this means for Piedmont Makers

**Our Fall 2026 LEGO League season will run as planned.** Registration for this fall is open ([sign up today!](/piedmontmakers.org/robotics)) and we're hosting a **Robotics Open House on Tuesday, March 24 at 7pm** for families to learn more — tell your friends. We can answer more questions there.

For 2027 and beyond, we'll evaluate options as both FIRST and LEGO Education share more details. Our commitment remains the same:

> To empower young people to be creative problem solvers and innovators in STEAM.

We will continue to offer the same level of quality, student-centered, community-based STEAM programs and we are excited about innovation in future seasons.

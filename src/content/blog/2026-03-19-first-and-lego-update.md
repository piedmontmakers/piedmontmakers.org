This morning FIRST and LEGO Education announced they're ending their partnership after nearly 30 years. The **2026–2027 season will be the last** they do together. After that, both organizations plan to go their own way, each developing independent robotics programs.

## What this means for us

**Our Fall 2026 LEGO League season will run as planned.** Registration is open for FLL Explore (K–3) and FLL Challenge (4–8). We're hosting a Robotics Open House on **Tuesday, March 24 at 7:00pm** for families to learn more — tell your friends.

For 2027 and beyond, we'll evaluate options as both FIRST and LEGO share more details about their independent plans. Our commitment doesn't change: we'll continue to offer the same level of quality, student-centered, community-based STEAM programs.

Whatever the two organizations decide separately, the kids in our programs will keep getting hands-on robotics. We're excited to see what each side comes up with — and we'll be ready to support whichever path serves Piedmont kids best.

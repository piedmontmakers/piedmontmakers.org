Registration is now open for the Piedmont Makers FIRST Tech Challenge Fall Season. The season runs from **August through February**.

FTC is for **grades 7–12** — [Register here](https://go.teamsnap.com/forms/518591).

Students from all across the East Bay are welcome to join.

We encourage working with your friends to establish pre-formed teams before registering. We will assist in forming/finding teams for individual registrants once registration has closed.

## Want to learn more?

Come to our **FTC Virtual Open House**, Tuesday May 5 at 8:00pm via [Google Meet](https://meet.google.com/hwd-dtri-tza).

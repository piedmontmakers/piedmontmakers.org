Registration is open for the Piedmont Makers FIRST Tech Challenge fall season. The program runs **August through February**, targeting students in grades 7–12. Students from across the East Bay are welcome to apply.

We recommend forming teams with friends before registering. If you're signing up individually, organizers will help with team placement once the registration window closes.

## Info session

We held a virtual info session on **May 5 at 8:00pm** via Google Meet. If you missed it, the presentation materials are posted on the [robotics page](/piedmontmakers.org/robotics) — scroll to the bottom for coach training and open-house resources.
